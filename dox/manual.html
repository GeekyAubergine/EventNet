Getting Started with EventNet

What is EventNet?

EventNet is a location based network. It allows you to communicate with other people who are at event's with you.

Why is it useful?

Say you're at a concert, and want to interact with other people at the concert. Just create or join and Event for the concert and away you go.

How to get started?

The first thing that you need to do it to find an event and join it by clicking on it. This will allow you to see and interact with other people at the same event as you, after this you can post and message people. You can choose to sign in using your Google ID at any time by going to settings and chosing Sign In. You can also choose to see what's happening in the Event from the Map view, this will allow you see where people are posting and commenting around you.
